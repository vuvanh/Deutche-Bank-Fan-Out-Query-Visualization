DENTONS BRAND STYLE GUIDE: COLOR PALETTES

Color palettes
The Dentons color palette is grounded firmly in the ownership of purple. The four colors below can be used together to build rich purple gradients and textures.

The secondary colors following can be used, in limited circumstances, to differentiate between brochures in the print system and between elements in complex charts. The five monochrome colors can be used as neutrals for line work and typography.

# Logo
Primary logo
Our logo with its directional guidepost, reinforces that we are committed to providing our clients with the guidance necessary to achieve the best solutions for their businesses. The logo points the way toward the future and communicates that we are forward-leaning, driven and visionary.

﻿It says we are a leader in reinventing the legal services industry, always with our clients’ best interests at heart.

All elements of the logo have been hand drawn and should be applied with care in accordance with these guidelines. ﻿'
